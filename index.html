FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long has Goldrich Printpak been in business?

Goldrich established its Canadian operations in 1953 and has served businesses across North America for more than 70 years.

Which industries do you serve?

The team specializes in food, pharmaceutical, cosmetics and alcohol packaging, along with custom printing and folding-carton projects.

What are the minimum order quantities?

The minimum order quantity is 10,000 units, allowing cost-effective custom production while maintaining high quality.

What printing and finishing capabilities are available?

Capabilities include offset and digital printing, specialty finishes, embossing, foil stamping and custom die-cutting.

Do you serve customers outside Canada?

Yes. Goldrich is based in Toronto and serves clients throughout North America.
