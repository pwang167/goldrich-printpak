Cosmetics packaging questions

Which premium finishes are available?

Options include embossing, debossing, foil stamping, spot UV, soft-touch and textured coatings, plus custom die-cut structures.

Can packaging support multiple product SKUs?

Yes. Goldrich can coordinate structure, colour, finish and inventory across product families, launches and staged rollouts.

How are regulatory needs handled?

Artwork and structures can account for date and batch coding, tamper evidence, theft prevention and cosmetics labelling requirements.
